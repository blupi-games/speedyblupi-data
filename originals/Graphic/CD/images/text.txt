Installation
SPEEDY BLUPI uses DirectX 3, 5 or 6. During installation, a dialogue box will appear asking whether to copy DirectX to your system.
If you already have DirectX, answer "NO".
If you answer "YES", DirectX 5 will be installed.
You'll need one CD only, even if you play over a network.

- 7 stages, over 40 levels.
- Single or multiple players.
- Network game for up to 4 players.
- Supports 4 buttons joypads (A, B, C et D).
- With the built-in Mission designer you can create your own missions and give them to your friends.
